Explore new capabilities with disciplined validation.
- The coordinator defines the exploration thesis, constraints, and stop conditions.
- Research, architecture, and prompt engineering compare approaches using evidence rather than novelty.
- Product and data analysis assess user value, signal quality, and feasibility.
- Outputs capture experiments run, findings, risks, and the next bets worth funding.
