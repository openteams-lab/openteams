Coordinate release readiness with one accountable command center.
- The coordinator owns scope, timeline, risk status, and go/no-go decisions.
- Quality verification proves the build against acceptance criteria and critical regressions.
- Documentation prepares release notes, operator guidance, and customer-facing changes.
- The team closes with launch evidence, monitoring notes, incidents, and follow-up owners.
