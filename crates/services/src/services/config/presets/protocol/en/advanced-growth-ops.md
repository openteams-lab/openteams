  description: Plan variants, audience split, channel setup, tracking, cadence, and responsible owners.
- title: Data interpretation
  description: Review quality of signal, attribution, segment movement, confidence, and unexpected effects.
- title: Decision and next action
  description: Choose scale, iterate, stop, or retest, then assign the next operating cycle.

Run growth operations as a disciplined experiment system.
- Product management owns the growth hypothesis, audience, decision threshold, and next action.
- Data analysis defines measurement, checks attribution quality, and explains performance movement.
- Marketing executes messaging, channel fit, and creative iteration without losing the experiment intent.
- Each cycle ends with a decision, learning record, and prioritized next experiment.
