- product_manager

- product_analyst
- data_analyst
- market_analyst
workflow_steps:
- title: Problem hypothesis and standards
  description: State the user problem, decision deadline, success criteria, and disconfirming signals.
- title: User and market evidence
  description: Collect interviews, behavioral data, market signals, and competing explanations.
- title: Minimum experiment and instrumentation
  description: Design the smallest useful test with tracking, sample boundaries, and risk controls.
- title: Prioritize insights and recommendations
  description: Rank findings by confidence and impact, then propose the next product decision.
tier: standard
enabled: true
---

Run discovery from evidence, not opinion.
- Product management frames the hypothesis, decision deadline, and success criteria.
- Design, product analytics, data, and market analysis separate observed signals from assumptions.
- The team converges on the smallest useful next experiment instead of broad speculative scope.
- Recommendations end with prioritized insights, open questions, and a measurement plan.
